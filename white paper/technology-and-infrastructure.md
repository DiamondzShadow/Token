### Technology and Infrastructure

## Blockchain Architecture

Diamondz Shadow utilizes Arbitrum Nitro technology to create Diamond zChain, a high-performance Layer 3 blockchain specifically designed for comprehensive media tokenization and community-driven entertainment.

### Core Components:

1. **Arbitrum Nitro Foundation**:

1. **Layer 3 Architecture**: Built on top of Arbitrum's proven Layer 2 infrastructure, providing specialized functionality for entertainment and gaming
2. **EVM Compatibility**: Full compatibility with Ethereum Virtual Machine, allowing seamless porting of existing smart contracts
3. **Data Availability**: Transaction data is efficiently compressed and stored with maximum security and verifiability
4. **Optimistic Rollup Technology**: Ensures security through fraud proofs while maintaining high throughput



2. **Custom Consensus Mechanism**:

1. **Proof of Contribution (PoC)**: Our innovative consensus mechanism that rewards users based on their contributions to the ecosystem
2. **Contribution Metrics**: Includes content creation, curation, promotion, governance participation, gaming achievements, and technical support
3. **Sybil Resistance**: Multi-factor verification system to prevent identity spoofing and contribution fraud
4. **Dynamic Reward Allocation**: Adjusts rewards based on ecosystem needs and contribution value



3. **Multi-Token Smart Contract Infrastructure**:

1. **SDM Contracts**: Custom gas token contracts optimized for high-frequency transactions
2. **TuBE Contracts**: ERC-20 contracts for content creation and community governance
3. **GaM3 Contracts**: Gaming-specific contracts with permit functionality for gasless approvals
4. **DuSTD Contracts**: Stablecoin contracts with price feed oracles for USD peg maintenance
5. **Content Registry**: On-chain registry of all content metadata, ownership, and revenue distribution rights
6. **Governance Framework**: DAO-based decision-making system with proposal, voting, and execution mechanisms
7. **Revenue Distribution**: Automated system for tracking and distributing revenue from multiple sources
8. **NFT Framework**: Specialized NFT contracts for content ownership, collectibles, and access rights
